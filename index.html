Skills

Magnetic Accelerator

All Critical Strike Chance is converted into Critical Strike Damage.

XQR Smart Round System

Fire agressive tracking rounds for 100% damage.

M99 Sniper

Activate your long-range scope, highlighting Weak Points and transforming your weapon into a piercing 1000% Damage railgun.

Concussion Device

Throw out a device that knocks back you and all nearby enemies. Can hold up to 2.

Supercharge

Fire a piercing round for 4000% damage and 150% Weak Point damage. Afterwards, all your weapons are disabled for 5 seconds.

I like that it's a spiritual successor to sniper while being its own character. Interested to see how backup mags will work with her or if it'll even do anything. But I think the long range focus will be a fun change.
